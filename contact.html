Frequently Asked Questions

Is Ayushman Card valid in K. Gurunath Cardiac Center Bhilai, Durg?
Yes, Ayushman Bharat Card is valid in K. Gurunath Cardiac Center Bhilai, Durg. The hospital is empaneled under the Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PMJAY) scheme, allowing eligible patients to receive cashless treatment without financial burden.
Which surgeries are covered under Ayushman Bharat in K. Gurunath Cardiac Center Bhilai?
Ayushman Bharat (PMJAY) covers various critical medical surgeries at our center, including:
  • ASD – Atrial Septal Defect Surgery
  • Heart Hole Repair
  • Single Chamber Permanent Pacemaker Implantation
  • Double Chamber Permanent Pacemaker Implantation
  • Abdominal Adhesion Surgery
  • Abdominal Myomectomy
  • Abdomino Perineal Repair for Mullerian Anomaly
  • Anterior & Posterior Colpoperineorrhaphy
  • Benign Abdominal Condition Surgery
  • Bladder Prolapse Repair
  • Bladder Support Surgery
  • Bladder-Vaginal Fistula Repair
  • Burch Procedure
  • Caesarean Delivery
What facilities are covered under Ayushman Card in K. Gurunath Cardiac Center Bhilai?
The Ayushman Card provides the following benefits:
  • Free Medical Treatment: Access to free treatments and hospital services.
  • Medical Insurance Cover: Health coverage up to ₹5 lakh per family per year.
  • Financial Protection: Protection against high medical expenses.
  • Wide Coverage: Covers various medical procedures and treatments.
  • Comprehensive Care: Includes treatment for multiple medical conditions.
How do I schedule an appointment?
You can schedule an appointment by contacting the hospital directly through their reception or official contact number during working hours.
What should I do in case of a cardiac emergency?
In case of a cardiac emergency, immediately visit the hospital emergency department or call local emergency services. Do not ignore symptoms such as chest pain, breathlessness, or severe discomfort.
