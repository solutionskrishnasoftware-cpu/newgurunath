Frequently asked questions

Is Ayushman Card valid in K. Gurunath Cardiac Center?
Yes, Ayushman Bharat Card is valid. The hospital is empaneled under PMJAY, allowing cashless treatment for eligible patients.
Which surgeries are covered under Ayushman Bharat?
It covers ASD, heart hole repair, pacemaker implants, myomectomy, and many more listed on our website.
What are the benefits of Ayushman Card?
Free treatment, insurance cover up to ₹5 lakh per family per year, financial protection against high expenses.
How do I schedule an appointment?
You can call us directly or use the appointment page on this site.
What should I do in a cardiac emergency?
Visit our emergency department immediately or call 0788 2294377. Do not ignore chest pain.
